RETURNING AN OBJECT

You can initiate a return within 15 days for eligible items; if you receive a wrong, damaged, defective or counterfeit item. For change of mind, a refund will only be processed if the seal of the item is still intact/ in no way tampered with. Orders shipped through Kenya Postal Services will not be eligible for return. When returning an item, ensure all seals, tags and accessories are left intact and item is in its original packaging. If you have created a password on the device you wish to return, please ensure it is removed, otherwise, your return will be invalid. Ensure all cases of wrong, damaged or missing items are reported within the shortest time possible after delivery/ collection to facilitate an easy return process. Get your refund faster when you drop off your return at the nearest Jumia dropoff station. Find below the table showing what you can return
